Laminated Cartons

Lamination stands out as the quintessential process for printing and packaging within India's market, notably revered in the Pharmaceutical sectors. Our offerings encompass a variety of Laminated Cartons, featuring options like PVC, Golden, Silver, Matt, and more.


Drip off Cartons

The drip-off effect, a cutting-edge technique in printing and packaging, has become a visual marvel in the Indian market, primarily embraced by the Cosmetic Industry. Its popularity has now extended to Pharmaceuticals and other industries as well.


UV Cartons

The UV effect emerged in the printing and packaging industry as a sustainable alternative to plastic film carton boxes following bans in Western and other nations. Its popularity has soared in recent years due to its glossy finish and radiant appeal, meeting significant demand.


Varnished Cartons

The varnish effect, a water-based enhancement, enhances the allure and durability of cartons with its vivid, luxurious, and smooth finish. It imparts a supremely professional appearance to your brand, making it highly sought after.


Embossed Cartons

For precise embossing on cartons, design intricate patterns or text, then use a detailed dry embossing technique to achieve fine details effectively.


Window Cartons

Window Cartons feature an opening sealed with transparent plastic film, designed to showcase the product within. Widely utilized in the Cosmetic Industry and FMCG products, they offer a visually engaging way to present merchandise.

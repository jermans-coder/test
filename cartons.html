Enhancing OTC Product Appeal with Custom Mono Cartons

Explore the Versatility and Compliance of Single-Layer Packaging



Mono cartons are single-layered carton boxes typically used for packaging various goods, including pharmaceuticals like OTC medications. Here are some key points about mono cartons in the context of OTC products:


Material and Structure: Mono cartons are usually made from a single layer of paperboard or cardboard. They are lightweight yet sturdy enough to protect the enclosed product.


Design and Customization: These cartons can be customized in terms of size, shape, and design to fit specific OTC products. They often feature printing for branding, product information, and regulatory requirements.


Usage: In the pharmaceutical industry, including OTC products, mono cartons are used to package tablets, capsules, syrups, and other forms of medications. They ensure product safety, hygiene, and compliance with industry standards.


Benefits: Mono cartons offer several advantages such as cost-effectiveness, recyclability (depending on the material used), and the ability to provide tamper-evident features for consumer safety.


Regulatory Compliance: Packaging for OTC products often needs to adhere to strict regulatory guidelines regarding information accuracy, safety warnings, and tamper resistance. Mono cartons can be designed to meet these requirements.
